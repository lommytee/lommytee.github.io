What I Do

I direct, write, edit and produce stories. I strive to make high-concept entertainment that is surreal, fun, and reminds us we determine our own mindset.

I interned at Prettybird (London, UK) to learn from music video professionals, the Oldenburg International Film Festival (Germany) to discover how great independent films get exhibited, and had a screenwriting masterclass doing reader’s reports for Brightlight Pictures (Vancouver, Canada). In 2017, I graduated from Capilano University’s Bachelor of Motion Picture Arts Program with the Dean’s Award of Excellence.

In the next three to five years I intend to write and direct my first feature film. Presently, I’m eager to direct commercials, music videos and short films.

If you like my work, I’d love to talk!
