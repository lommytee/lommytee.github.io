Hi, I'm Tommy Lee

What I Do

I direct, write, edit and produce stories. I love film that entertains us in the narrow margin between absurd and profound, reminding us to smile. I'm inspired by drama, surrealism, comedy, sci-fi and all their hybrid genres. My current focus is to hone short narratives, expand my community of creative friends and move towards feature films.

What I've Done

In 2017, I graduated from Capilano University’s Bachelor of Motion Picture Arts Program with the Dean’s Award of Excellence. Between academic years, I directed a trilogy of award-winning music videos for the band Good For Grapes. My film's have screened at NFFTY, The Whistler Film Festival, The Los Angeles Music Video Festival and The Chicago International Film Festival. I have interned at Prettybird (London, UK) to learn from music video professionals, the Oldenburg International Film Festival (Germany) to discover how great independent films get exhibited, and had a screenwriting masterclass doing reader’s reports for Brightlight Pictures (Vancouver, Canada).

What's Next?

I’m developing a webseries that examines the humour and drama of daily life, set 20 years in the future. I’m also working on a VR narrative that explores how people react to the unknown.

If you like my work, I’d love to talk!
